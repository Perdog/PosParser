POS Parser Trying to make this shit easier.

How to use:
- When on grid with a POS, change the D-Scan unit from AU to KM (by clicking on the letters).
- Set distance to something short that won't pick up anything BUT the POS (200 KM should work).
- Scan.
- Click on the top result in the D-scan window, press CTRL+A, then CTRL+C. Open this window and paste into the box. Press "Parse" and you're done :D
